Donald Stephens

Donald's focus diverted from sequential art as he undertook a new passion for what is now the Treehouse Series. An idea brought to life with passion for fantasy and the classic tale of Peter Pan's lost boys hiding in elaborate treehouses. All painted by hand with acrylic on old reused windows these paintings find intricate ways of merging the frail almost impossible treehouse architecture with our imagination. Donald Stephens is a Chicago based Illustrator/Painter.
