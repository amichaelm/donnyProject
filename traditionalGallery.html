Traditional Work

It all started here with traditional art and continues to do so. All of my work starts with drawn ideas, sketches, and/or writing. Only some of the work I do traditionally is crystal clean like my digital art. My traditional work is messy, dirty, in a complete disarray, and that's exactly how I like it and how it will stay. My process includes looking at tons of different things and letting my mind wander to wherever it likes before I take the work and upload it onto the computer, paint it onto a window, or apply it to a canvas.
